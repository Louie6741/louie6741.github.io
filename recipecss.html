Brownie Recipe
My grandma has been making brownies for many years. She has made them for my dad and his brothers and now for me and my cousins. The brownies are very cakey, while still being fudgy and crispy enough. Every time we have a family dinner my grandma makes and brings the brownies for a dessert.
Recipe Background
This brownie recipe is similar to my grandma's recipe and it is from the website LoveandLemons.
Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F. Spray an 8×8-inch pan with cooking spray.
  2. Line with parchment paper, and spray again.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, oil, water, and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Sprinkle the dry ingredients over the wet ingredients and stir just until combined.
  6. Pour the thick batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 40–50 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  8. Let cool completely for at least 2 hours before slicing.
  9. Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days or freeze for longer storage.
