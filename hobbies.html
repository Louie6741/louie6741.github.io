Louis Brosnan's Childhood Hobbies

At a young age one of my closest friends at the time had gotten me into soccer. I soon fell in love with the sport. I joined the club Kics where my friends played at the time. After a year or so I transferred to club Chicago City where I made the top team. At the end of sixth grade I began to lose my love for the sport and eventually quit. Up until 2025 summer where new friends helped me find my love for the sport. After lots of summer training I was able to make Lane Tech's Frosh B team.

During freshman year I found out about the art of urbexing, which is exploring abandoned locations. For a while, me and my friends planned to go urbexing, but we never ended up going. The truth is we never knew where to go. Up until a week ago when my two friends and I heard of this abandoned gallery, we then checked the place out and had a ton of fun exploring it and taking pictures. We plan on urbexing many more times.

Everyone loves movies, whether it's a comedy, drama, horror, or action movie. My dad always really liked them and wanted to be a director. From a young age he showed me very good movies. I loved all of the Star Wars and Marvel movies. As I got older, he started to show me more mature movies like Goodfellas, Good Will Hunting, and many more. If I were to choose my top five movies right now on January 20, 2026, I would say: 1. Goodfellas 2. Wall-E 3. Dark Knight 4. Uncut Gems 5. Boyhood.

I have played video games my entire life. I started playing games on my Nintendo Switch. Then one Christmas I got a PS4, and for the next 2 years I played on that. Up until Christmas 2021, when I got the brand new PS5. If I were to choose my top five video games ever right now on January 20, 2026, I would say: 1. Fortnite 2. Madden 3. Call of Duty 4. GTA 5 5. NBA2K.
