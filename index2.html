About Louis Brosnan

Hello! My name is Louis Brosnan. I was born on March 25, 2011, in Los Angeles, California, to my parents, John Brosnan and Kara Blinn. Two years later, my family and I moved to Chicago, where we have have lived ever since. When I was almost four years old, my younger brother Eddie was born. At age five, I began attending LaSalle II, starting in kindergarten. During my time there, I made many new friends, including Orlando and Chase. Chase introduced me to soccer, a sport I grew passionate about and played throughout my time at LaSalle. In sixth grade, I was accepted into the academic center at William Howard Taft. I attended Taft for seventh and eighth grade, where I made many new friends. By the end of eighth grade, I decided it was time for a change and transferred to Lane Tech for high school. I am currently a ninth-grade student at Lane Tech High School. I have made many new friends while keeping many old ones, made the freshman soccer team, and participate in several clubs, including Mukbang and Business. Throughout my life I try to live by the quote "That doesn't even enter my consiousness" (Timothee Chalamet).
