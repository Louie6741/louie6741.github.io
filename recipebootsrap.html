Brownie Recipe

Grandma's Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F. Spray an 8×8-inch pan with cooking spray.
  2. Line with parchment paper, and spray again.
  3. Whisk together the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  4. Whisk the eggs, oil, water, and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet and stir until just combined.
  6. Pour batter into the pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 40–50 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs.
  8. Cool completely for at least 2 hours before slicing.
  9. Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
